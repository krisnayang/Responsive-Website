Our history

Western food, especially steaks had been a common and popular meal for Indonesian, especially in big cities like in Jakarta. At that time most of the restaurants concepts were purely high-priced, fine dining, which would normally found in countries around the world. The people’s point of view at that time was that steaks were food for the rich. It brought us an idea to serve different varieties of steaks and western food with excellent quality in affordable price for everyone to enjoy.

This idea was then realized with the opening of the first AbabaSteak outlet on June 2005 in Jakarta. AbabaSteak comes with the concept of a family restaurant, that will make you feel good while enjoying every bite of the mouth-watering steak. Currently, AbabaSteak is available in the cities of Jakarta, Surabaya, Bandung, Medan, and Palembang.
